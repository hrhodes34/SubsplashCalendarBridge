How to Get Real Calendar IDs

  1. Go to Google Calendar
  2. In the left sidebar, find your calendars
  3. Click the three dots (â‹®) next to each calendar name
  4. Select "Settings and sharing"
  5. Scroll down to "Integrate calendar"
  6. Copy the Calendar ID (it should look like example@gmail.com or example@group.calendar.google.com)

Common Calendar IDs:

  • primary - Your main Google Calendar
  • your.email@gmail.com - Your personal calendar
    • example@group.calendar.google.com - A shared/group calendar
